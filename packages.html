Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our internet packages

What's included in the installation fee?

Our installation includes all necessary equipment (router/modem), setup, configuration, and basic testing. For standard installations, there's no additional fee for the first month. Special installations may have additional charges which we discuss upfront.

Is there a contract or lock-in period?

No! We believe in service quality. There are no long-term contracts. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your service with 30 days' notice. We're confident you'll stay because of our excellent service, not because of a contract.

What happens if the internet goes down?

We offer 24/7 technical support. If your internet goes down, call us immediately at 0706 315 742. Our average response time is under 30 minutes, and we guarantee to resolve most issues within 4 hours. Business packages include priority support with guaranteed 2-hour response time.
