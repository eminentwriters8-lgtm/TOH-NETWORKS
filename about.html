How It Began

Our Foundation Story

Founded: October 12, 2025
We started with a simple vision: to bring reliable internet connectivity to every home and business in Kibwezi.

Toh Networks was born out of a recognized need in our community. In 2025, Kibwezi was experiencing rapid growth but faced significant challenges in digital connectivity. Many businesses struggled with unreliable internet, students faced difficulties accessing online resources, and homes lacked proper security systems.

Our founder, Martin Mutinda Mbiti, identified this gap and assembled a team of passionate technology experts. We began with modest resources but with a big dream: to transform Kibwezi into a digitally connected community where everyone could access reliable internet and feel secure in their homes and businesses.

Key Moments in Our Journey:

October 12, 2025: Toh Networks officially launched in Kibwezi
November 2025: Connected first 50 customers
December 2025: Expanded to include CCTV and security services
January 2026: Reached 100+ satisfied customers milestone

Today, we're proud to be Kibwezi's most trusted network solutions provider. But our mission remains the same: to connect, secure, and empower our community through technology.
